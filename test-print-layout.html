How to test the print layout:

  1. Click one of the song buttons below to load a song sheet
  2. Use Ctrl+P (or Cmd+P on Mac) to open print preview
  3. Check that:
    • Navigation buttons are hidden
    • Content fits on one page
    • Chord diagrams are readable and centered
    • Chord colors show correctly: C G F Am
    • Colors appear both on screen AND in print
    • Small URL and branding appear at the bottom

Color Scheme: Red = C, Green = G, Yellow = F, Blue = Am
